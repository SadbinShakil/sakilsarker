Sakil Sarker

I am a master's student at Ontario Tech University (OTU) Computer Science Program, specializing in Artificial Intelligence. Under the guidance of Dr. Kourosh Davoudi and Dr. Ali Neshati, I am actively engaged in research focused on Human-AI Interaction and Large Language Models (LLMs). Additionally, I serve as both a Teaching Assistant and Graduate Research Assistant here at OTU.

Prior to my master's studies, I earned a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from East West University (EWU), where I also held the position of Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (UTA). Subsequently, I worked as a Software Engineer at BJIT Ltd. During my undergraduate studies, I gained valuable experience as a Deep Learning Research Intern at Carnegie Mellon University, where I collaborated with Dr. Min Xu and Mostofa Rafid Uddin on leveraging machine translation for protein structure prediction.
